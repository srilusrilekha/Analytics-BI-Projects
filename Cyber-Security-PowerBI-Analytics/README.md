# Enterprise Cyber Security Power BI Analytics

## Project Overview

This project delivers a centralized Power BI reporting solution designed to provide executive and operational visibility into enterprise cybersecurity metrics across governance, risk, compliance, vulnerability management, and security operations.

The solution transforms curated datasets from an AWS-based cloud data platform into interactive dashboards, enabling stakeholders to monitor cyber posture, track KPIs, analyze trends, and support data-driven decision-making through a standardized reporting experience.

## Business Problem

Enterprise cybersecurity metrics were distributed across multiple operational systems, making it difficult to provide consistent and executive-level reporting.

Key business challenges included:

- Fragmented cybersecurity reporting
- Multiple disconnected reporting sources
- Lack of standardized KPI definitions
- Manual reporting efforts
- Limited executive visibility
- Inconsistent business logic across reports

A centralized reporting solution was required to provide consistent, interactive, and business-friendly analytics.

---

## Solution Overview

The solution consumes curated datasets from the enterprise cloud data platform and transforms them into interactive Power BI dashboards through semantic modeling, DAX calculations, and visualization best practices.

The reporting solution provides:

- Executive KPI reporting
- Governance and compliance reporting
- Security operations monitoring
- Interactive business analytics
- Operational scorecards
- Drill-through analysis
- Business unit and department filtering

# Confidentiality Notice

This repository is a portfolio representation of an enterprise Power BI reporting solution.

To protect confidential business information:

- Corporate branding has been removed or sanitized.
- Sensitive business metrics have been generalized where appropriate.
- Representative dashboards are included in place of the complete reporting application.
- Business-specific calculations, DAX expressions, and confidential datasets have been simplified.
- The semantic model has been sanitized while preserving the overall design approach.

The objective of this repository is to demonstrate enterprise Power BI architecture, semantic modeling, dashboard development, and reporting best practices while maintaining client confidentiality.

Thank you for your understanding and respect for client confidentiality.
