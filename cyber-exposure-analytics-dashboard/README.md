# Business Problem

Large organizations often rely on multiple operational systems to manage business processes, risk monitoring, and performance tracking. As data grows across different platforms, stakeholders face challenges in obtaining a unified and accurate view of organizational performance.

Common challenges include:

* Data scattered across multiple systems
* Manual report preparation
* Inconsistent KPI calculations
* Limited visibility into trends and performance
* Delayed decision-making due to fragmented reporting

## Objective

The goal of this project was to design an interactive analytics solution that provides centralized visibility into key business metrics and performance indicators.

The solution enables:

* Executive-level performance monitoring
* Trend and variance analysis
* KPI tracking against targets
* Operational insights through drill-down capabilities
* Improved data-driven decision-making

## Expected Outcomes

* Single source of truth for reporting
* Reduced manual reporting effort
* Improved stakeholder visibility
* Faster access to actionable insights
* Enhanced reporting consistency across teams
